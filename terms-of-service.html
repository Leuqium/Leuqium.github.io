2. PRODUCT & TIME OF COMPLETION

The product will be digital, and will be a .png file (+ additional files if needed) with an image size based on the client personal preference (If not specified it is not less than 4000x4000 pixel), compressed to a .rar file scanned on VirusTotal. A .psd file will be included for an additional fee (See Section 3 about Pricing).

The estimated time of completion (ETC) for your commission can be up to 3 months depending on many factor such as:

  1. Personal circumstances (Health, university, family issues);
  2. Work (Commission order and priority, personal works);

I will be notifying the client about the progress of the drawing and asking for opinions, and it is in the other party’s best interest to be communicative as much as possible.

Any request regarding the commission's time of completion, without priority fee, will be ignored. Frequently asking for the time of completion is discouraged and will also be ignored. It is possible to check an estimate of the next revision on the spreadsheet provided in the website.


3. PRICING

The prices of digital products are as quoted in my social from time to time, and discussed with the client.

Prices are subject to change at any time, but changes will not affect commissions in respect of which I’m already working on. Be aware that I have the right to modify them at any time, including during negotiations.

Additional fees may be applied on commission complexity and difficulty, and are different based on illustration subject (i.e.; Anthropomorphic, Mechanical, Non-humanoid etc.).

If you request your finished final commission to not be posted and be kept private there may be a privacy fee.

The client is allowed to a limited number of changes during the sketch phase, and three minor changes during the artwork's rendering (i.e. coloring and painting) and completion.

Any additional request, regarding the current commission, will be charged with an arbitrary fee in which, cumulatevely, do not surpass the 75% of the base price. Any additional request, outside the remaining changes permitted during revisions, and outside the commission in progress, will be placed on the last available slot on the queue.

PSD file is available for 15% of total price.

You can buy commercial use for you commission for the 250% of total price.


4. PAYMENT TERMS & REFUND POLICY

Payment will be processed through PayPal only. Full payment must be received prior to the start of the drawing, and it is not refundable after an invoice has been sent, unless under circumstances such as:

  1. Cancellation made by me, the artist. In this case a full refund will be given, regardless of the product’s progress;
  2. Cancellation asked by the commissioner, in which a partial refund will be given based on the progress of the drawing, plus an additional fee of 10% of total price for the request to cancel.

Partial refund shall not exceed 90% of the total price.
